About Us

We are a Ghana based ushering agency providing services to Ghana, Africa and beyond.

Our aim is to provide trained ushers who deliver exceptional services for any event.

The main goal of CARLEX Ghana Limited is to make sure that the guests of our clients are treated with respect and hospitality to ensure comfortability during the course of the event. CARLEX Ghana Limited provides nothing but the best of the best; Ushers who have been specially trained in the art of hospitality and serve effectively as exhibition assistants, for tv commercials, also as events hostesses for private parties and activations, weddings, corporate programs
